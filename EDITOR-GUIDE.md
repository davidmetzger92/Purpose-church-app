# Editing the Purpose App — Quick Guide

This is how to change the buttons, links, and service times in the Purpose app.
You can't break the layout — you're only editing content. Takes a minute.

---

## First time only (about 5 minutes)

1. **Make a free GitHub account** at https://github.com/signup
   - You can use your normal (Google) email address.
   - Write down your username and password somewhere safe.
2. **Accept the invite.** David will send you an email invite to the project —
   open it and click **Accept**. (This is what lets your changes save.)

That's it for setup. You only do this once.

---

## To make a change (every time)

1. Go to the editor:
   **https://davidmetzger92.github.io/Purpose-church-app/admin/**
   *(Keep this link to yourself — it's the staff editor, not the public app.)*
2. Click **Sign In with GitHub** and approve.
3. Click **App Content** → **Home Screen**.
4. Change what you need:
   - **Service Times** — the times shown at the top.
   - **Location** — the place name, address, and map link.
   - **Give Link** — the online giving page.
   - **Quick-Link Buttons** — add, remove, reorder, or rename the buttons.
     Each button has a **name**, an **icon** (pick from the dropdown), and a
     **link**.
5. Click **Save** (top-right).
6. Wait about a minute, then open/refresh the app to see your change.

---

## Good to know

- **You can't break it.** You're only editing text and links — the design and
  layout are locked. Worst case, a typo in a link; just fix it and Save again.
- **Icons** come from a dropdown so you can't mistype them.
- **Links** open in the browser when tapped — that's normal.
- **Adding a button:** under Quick-Link Buttons, click the **+ Add** button,
  fill in the name, pick an icon, paste the link, then Save.
- **Stuck or unsure?** Don't force it — text David before changing anything
  you're not confident about.
