Editing the App

A quick guide to updating buttons, links, and service times.

Takes about a minute
First time only · ~5 min

Get set up

You only do these two things once.

1
Make a free GitHub account at github.com/signup.
You can use your normal email. Save your username and password somewhere safe.
2
Accept the invite. David will email you an invite to the project — open it and click Accept.
This is what lets your changes save. Without it, edits won't stick.
Every time

Make a change

Open the editor, change what you need, hit Save.

1
Open the editor and click Sign In with GitHub.
Keep the editor link to yourself — it's the staff door, not the public app.
2
Click App Content → Home Screen.
3
Change what you need (see below), then click Save in the top-right.
4
Wait about a minute, then open or refresh the app to see it.
Already-open apps update on the next refresh.

The fields

What you can edit

Everything on the app's home screen, in one form.

Service Times

The times shown at the top of the app.

Location

Place name, address, and the map link.

Give Link

Where the "Give online" button goes.

Quick-Link Buttons

Add, remove, reorder, or rename buttons. Each has a name, an icon (from a dropdown), and a link.

Good to know

You can't break it

A few things that make this stress-free.

You're only editing text and links — the design and layout are locked. Worst case is a typo; just fix it and Save again.
Icons come from a dropdown, so you can't mistype them. To add a button, use + Add under Quick-Link Buttons.
Stuck or unsure? Don't force it — text David before changing anything you're not confident about.
